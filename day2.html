HTML Entities, Symbols and Emojis

Entities, symbols and emojis have their own syntax which must be used for them to display in text elements.

Without the correct syntax the browser may confuse entities as tags.

"< ;" is the syntax for < (minus the space)

Other characters such as symbols dont have keys on most keyboards so you have to use their decimal or hexadecimal references

"&euro ;" is the syntax for € (minus the space)

Emojis are not images, They are letters (characters) from the UTF-8 (Unicode) character set which covers most characters and symbols in the world

"& #128511;" is the syntax for 🗿 (minus the space)
